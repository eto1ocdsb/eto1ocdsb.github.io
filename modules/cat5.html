My experience with AP computer science: programming

The AP computer science course tackles the fundamentals and logic of computer science in the form of pseudocode. These foundational concepts built into the concept helped me and will continue to help me in future computer science classes. The aspects that helped me most as a student were conditionals with if, else, and booleans, which were used in future lessons such as those by Dan Shiffman.
